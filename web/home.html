FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions about setup, privacy, and automatic job application tracking.

Does Applictus have access to my Gmail inbox?

No. Applictus does not request Gmail inbox read permissions. Google is used only for authentication. Job updates are received through forwarding that you control and can disable at any time.

What job boards does Applictus support?

Applictus works with job-related emails from virtually any job board, company career site, or hiring platform. If application updates arrive in your inbox, Applictus can organize them into your application timeline.

How does Applictus track my applications?

Applictus automatically organizes forwarded job emails into a timeline of applications, interviews, offers, rejections, and other hiring updates.

Can Applictus detect interview requests?

Yes. Applictus can identify interview invitations, assessments, recruiter outreach, offers, rejections, and other hiring events from supported job-related emails.

Can I stop forwarding emails anytime?

Yes. You remain in control of your forwarding settings and can disable forwarding whenever you choose.

Is Applictus free?

Yes. Applictus offers a free plan so you can start organizing your job search without paying upfront. Upgrade only if you need additional tracking capacity.
