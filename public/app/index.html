Privacy Policy

	          
Last updated: Feb 2026

	        

	        

	          

	            This policy explains how Applictus handles your information. It may evolve as the product grows.
	          


	          
Overview

	          

	            Applictus helps you track job applications by connecting Gmail and organizing job‑related updates. We
	            access only what we need to deliver these features and keep your data confined to your account.
	          


	          
Gmail Read‑Only Access

	          

	            When you connect Gmail, Applictus uses read‑only access to analyze job‑related messages.
	            We do not send email, delete email, or modify your mailbox.
	          


	          
Data We Access

	          
    
	            
  • Account information: email address, name (optional), and sign‑in method.
    • 
	            
  • 
	              Gmail metadata: sender, subject, snippet, timestamps, message and thread identifiers,
	              and labels needed for deduping and status inference.
	            
    • 
	            
  • 
	              Message content (limited): when required to detect job‑related signals and classify
	              updates. Access remains read‑only.
	            
    • 
	            
  • 
	              Derived application data: company, role/title, and inferred status signals that power
	              your dashboard.
	            
    • 
	            
  • 
	              OAuth tokens: used to access Gmail (stored encrypted when encryption is enabled).
	            
    • 
	          


	          
How We Use Data

	          
    
	            
  • Detect job‑related emails and keep application statuses current.
    • 
	            
  • Prevent duplicates by tracking message identifiers.
    • 
	            
  • Provide context for inferred updates where available.
    • 
	            
  • Maintain reliability and security (dedupe, fraud prevention, debugging).
    • 
	          


	          
What We Do Not Do

	          
    
	            
  • We do not sell your data.
    • 
	            
  • We do not show ads or use your data for ad targeting.
    • 
	            
  • We do not allow human review of your Gmail content unless you explicitly ask for support.
    • 
	          


	          
Google API Limited Use Disclosure

	          

	            Applictus’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API
	            Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
	          


	          
Retention

	          

	            We retain tracking data while your account is active. If you disconnect Gmail, Applictus stops reading
	            your mailbox. Previously extracted tracking data remains in your account unless you request deletion.
	          


	          
Security

	          
    
	            
  • We use HTTPS in production.
    • 
	            
  • OAuth tokens are stored encrypted when encryption is enabled.
    • 
	            
  • Access is tied to your authenticated session.
    • 
	          


	          
Third‑Party Services

	          
    
	            
  • Google OAuth / Gmail: authentication and permitted read‑only mailbox access.
    • 
	            
  • Hosting provider: runs the Applictus service.
    • 
	            
  • Database provider: stores account and tracking data.
    • 
	          


	          
Your Controls

	          
    
	            
  • Disconnect Gmail from the Account page at any time.
    • 
	            
  • Log out to clear your session on this device.
    • 
	            
  • If account/data deletion is not available in‑app yet, contact us and we’ll help.
    • 
	          


	          
Contact

	          

	            Questions? Email support@applictus.com.

Terms of Service

	          
Last updated: Feb 2026

	        

	        

	          
Overview

	          

	            Applictus helps you track job applications by organizing job‑related updates in a dashboard. These terms
	            explain how you may use the service and how we operate it.
	          


	          
Gmail Connection (Optional)

	          

	            Connecting Gmail is optional. If you connect, Applictus uses read‑only access to detect
	            job‑related messages and keep your application statuses current. You can disconnect Gmail at any time
	            from the Account page.
	          


	          
How the Service Works

	          

	            Applictus automatically groups and classifies job‑related updates. We provide best‑effort inferences based
	            on the email data you authorize. You control final decisions and any edits to your records.
	          


	          
Account Responsibilities

	          
    
	            
  • Keep your account credentials secure and accurate.
    • 
	            
  • Use the service only with data you own or are authorized to access.
    • 
	            
  • Review and confirm important application details when needed.
    • 
	          


	          
Acceptable Use

	          
    
	            
  • No abuse, automated scraping, or attempts to bypass security controls.
    • 
	            
  • No interference with the service, including excessive load or spam.
    • 
	          


	          
Termination

	          

	            We may suspend or terminate access if we believe your use violates these terms or harms the service. You
	            may stop using Applictus at any time.
	          


	          
Changes

	          

	            We may update these terms from time to time. If changes are material, we’ll make a reasonable effort to
	            notify you. The “Last updated” date reflects the latest version.
	          


	          
Contact

	          

	            Questions? Email support@applictus.com.

About Applictus

			            

			              Applictus was built to reduce the chaos and fragmentation of the job search so people can track real progress without adding more tools to manage.

Why we built it

			            

			              We kept seeing the same pattern: job searches spread across inbox threads, spreadsheets, notes, and browser tabs, making it hard to understand real momentum. Applictus was created to simplify that experience, not add complexity, so people can spend less time managing process and more time pursuing opportunities.
			            

			          

			
			          

			            
How we approach Applictus

			            

			              

			                
Clarity over complexity

			                
We prioritize interfaces and decisions that make progress easier to read at a glance.

			              

			              

			                
Automation where it matters

			                
We automate repetitive tracking work so users can focus on applications and conversations.

			              

			              

			                
User control and flexibility

			                
Users decide what flows in, how it is configured, and when to change their setup.

			              

			            

			          

			
			          

			            
Privacy and control

			            

			              Trust starts with clear boundaries and user control, so Applictus is designed to stay transparent and flexible.
			            

			            

			              

			                
You control what gets forwarded

			                
Choose which emails are sent to your Applictus inbox and adjust those rules when needed.

			              

			              

			                
You can disconnect anytime

			                
Disconnect Gmail at any time while keeping your existing timeline accessible in Applictus.

			              

			              

			                
Access stays session-protected

			                
Your account remains tied to authenticated sessions and your selected sign-in method.

			              

			            

			          

			
			          

			            

			              Applictus exists to make the job search feel more manageable, not more overwhelming.
